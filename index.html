Rapid Iteration.

Think introspectively about a problem in your daily life that you haven't had any time to fix. Others are in the same predicament elsewhere. What's the quickest solution?

Great, now Where does that fall short? Longevity of materials? Strength of joints? Make another iteration.

Better, what if someone else where to find your solution in place? Would they use it the same way?

This Year's Theme

Solving Day-to-Day Problems


Throughout our day to day lives we, as individual, experience a variety of problems that range on the scale of impact and importance. However, the thought/emotion that often comes to mind when these problems arise is the desire for a solution for them!

Today we will finally take the initiative and create potential fixes to these issues we have to joy of encountering on a daily basis. Think of some issue you may currently be annoyed with. (such as hair getting tangled in your comb, blow drying not strong enough, etc. whatever you can think of!)

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to be a design student to participate?

No! RISD Make-A-Thon is an interdisciplinary event for students from diverse backgrounds.

Who can attend?

All undergraduate and graduate students interested in solving problems are welcome!

Will there be food provided?

Yes! RISD Make-A-Thon will provide delicious meals for all makers who show up to the event. Free food, drinks, supplies, and exclusive swags will be provided.

What is IDSA?

RISD IDSA is a student chapter for the nonprofit Industrial Designers Society of America (IDSA), which is one of the oldest and largest membership associations for industrial design professionals.

IDSA RISD chapter strives to coordinate events that expose students to different professional fields of not only industrial design, but also interdisciplinary design.
